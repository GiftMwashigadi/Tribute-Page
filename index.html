Here's a timeline of Charlie Kirk’s life:

  • 1993 – Born in the Chicago area, Illinois, USA.
  • 2011 – Graduates from high school and enrolls at Marian Central Catholic High School in Woodstock, Illinois.
  • 2012 – Founds Turning Point USA, a nonprofit organization aimed at promoting conservative values and free markets on high school and college campuses.
  • 2013 – Gains national attention as Turning Point USA rapidly expands, establishing chapters across U.S. colleges.
  • 2016 – Becomes a prominent conservative speaker and commentator, known for his strong support of free speech and conservative political activism.
  • 2017 – Publishes his first book, "Campus Battlefield", outlining his vision for conservative activism in higher education.
  • 2018 – Expands Turning Point USA’s reach with youth conferences and campus events, growing the organization’s influence significantly.
  • 2019 – Launches Turning Point Action, the political action arm of his movement, aiming to mobilize young conservative voters.
  • 2020 – Plays a key role in conservative media and political campaigns, frequently appearing on national news outlets and social media.
  • 2021 – Continues to be a leading voice in conservative activism, emphasizing issues such as free speech, limited government, and traditional values.
  • 2023 – Remains a central figure in youth conservative movements, hosting conferences and growing his digital media presence.
  • 2025 - dies at the age of 31.
